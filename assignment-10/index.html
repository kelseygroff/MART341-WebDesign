Trends in the 80s

Fashion trends in the 80s consisted of big hair, high waisted jeans, denim jackets, neon colors, leg warmers, and more. Simply, it was considered the bolest decade in modern fashion history. This particular fashion was influenced by numerous celebrities such as Michael Jackson, Madonna, and Princess Diana that were the trend setters for this particular generation.
Entertainment trends in the 80s played an important role in what the 80s were. There were dozens of movies and television series that revolved around current events during that time. People spent their time off listening to CDs, renting and watching Blockbuster videos, going to the arcade, and more. Needless to say, the 1980s were a time of great pop culture and is still referenced to this day.
